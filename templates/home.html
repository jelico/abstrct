Linux Campus Club - SJCE

Linux Campus Club is an organization under the department of Computer Science SJCE Mysore India, with a primary goal of fostering the use of free open source software among the students.It was started back in 2004. In this endeavor, LCC was founded with following goals:

  • Advocating the adoption of FOSS instead of proprietary software.
  • Educating and training members to use GNU/Linux and associated components.
  • Providing support and guidance for new users.
  • Creating an environment which facilitates free discussion leading to sharing of ideas and knowledge.
